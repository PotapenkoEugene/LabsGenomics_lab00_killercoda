# Welcome to the Server

You are sitting at your home computer — `controlplane`.

Your genomics course runs on a remote server called `labserver`. It has 64 GB of RAM and 2 TB of disk — because a single sequencing run can produce 15–100 GB of data, which your laptop cannot handle.

To work with genomics data, you need to connect to `labserver` over the network using **SSH**.

In this exercise you will:
1. See who you are and where you are on `controlplane`
2. SSH into `labserver`
3. See how your identity and location change on the server
